Our Story

Grain's Mart began with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between local farmers and urban families. Founded in 2020 by agricultural enthusiasts, we recognized the need for a direct, transparent supply chain that benefits both producers and consumers.

What started as a small initiative with just 5 partner farms has now grown into a network of over 100 farms across the region, serving thousands of families with farm-fresh products delivered right to their doorsteps.
